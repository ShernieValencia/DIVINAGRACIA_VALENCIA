Our Curated Date Ideas

A glimpse into the diverse experiences we can create.

"Progressive Dinner" Night

Instead of eating at one restaurant, have each course (appetizers, main, dessert) at a different location. This keeps the evening moving, creates anticipation, and allows you to try multiple venues.

Stargazing with Hot Drinks

Drive to a spot away from city lights (a park or hilltop). Bring a blanket, a thermos of hot cocoa/cider/tea, and perhaps a star chart app. It's a peaceful, intimate date focused on quiet appreciation.

Recreate Your First Date

A nostalgic and sweet tribute to your relationship's beginning. Go back to the original spot, order the same thing, and reminisce about how you both felt that first time. It's a wonderful way to reconnect.
